About Skky High Flight Bookings

Skky High Flight is a new and vibrant online flight booking site with exciting and more travelling services to offer. Skky High Flight Bookings was established in 2006 and has been operating since 2007. This exciting innovation was found by Mthokozisi Mahlangu and Don Sibiya in partnership with The Innovation Hub. Skky High Flight Bookings has physical offices at The Innovation Hub but it is a fully functional online website that provides the ability for a client to book a plane ticket online and take a flight at any time that the client has picked.
